My ratings are subjective, based mainly on taste,
since some prices I don't remember.

## Rankings

1. Xinfutang -- 5/5. I tried this in NYC and the boba pearls tasted
   really fresh, along with good tasting tea.
2. Tea Top -- 5/5. I might be biased since my girlfriend works there,
   but the tea tastes fresh and good quality, and drinks are consistent.
   The matcha is good as well.
