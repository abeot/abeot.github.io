## Books I've read

- The Art of Thinking Clearly: rate 5/5, very enjoyable read
  about how we think and our biases. Highly recommend!
- Atomic Habits: rate 3.5/5, it gets repetitive and some things feel
  obvious, but it was enjoyable
- Sapiens: rate 5/5, very enjoyable read about human history. 
  Easy to understand and uses reason and recognize
  nuances. Highly recommend!
- Outliers: rate 4/5, it gets repetitive but also was eye-opening
  to me about building success.
- The Martian: rate 5/5, felt predictable but well-told and funny
  science fiction novel. Highly recommend the book! (Haven't watched the movie)
